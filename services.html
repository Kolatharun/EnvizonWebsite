Poised to disrupt the event lead capture landscape, Popl needed a more mature brand to match its vision — without sacrificing the cutting-edge spirit of its team.

Brand Strategy

The Magical Ruler guides its industry as the clear leader while facilitating awe-inspiring experiences for its audience, empowering them to unlock their next level.
We worked with Popl to develop a brand concept, a big, aspirational theme rooted in brand strategy: “Connection that clicks” just, well, clicked. During in-person meetings, there’s a connection. It’s not just about the lead, it’s about the relationship. Popl customers can get there first and build that rapport, facilitating a customer experience that feels like magic. With Popl, leads spark connection. Stronger relationships, partnerships, and conversations — all primed to last.
Combining the Ruler archetype — that of an industry leader — with the Magician brought us to a custom persona to guide our work with Popl: the Magical Ruler. This archetype creates a world of structure and stability for its audience, serving as a powerful and trustworthy leader and helping customers tap into new abilities.

Logo

The Popl logotype represents the seamless and simplified experience of creating connections. The flowing linkage between the counters of the letters symbolizes the ease of connecting with others.
That flow is then balanced with highly geometric and technical type design, giving this logo the cutting-edge feel we were after.
